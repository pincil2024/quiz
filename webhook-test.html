Enter your Lynk.id webhook secret below. This will be encrypted and stored in your browser.

If you don't provide one, a secure key will be generated for you

Where to Find Your Webhook Secret

  1. Log in to your Lynk.id merchant dashboard
  2. Go to Settings → Webhooks
  3. Find your webhook configuration
  4. Look for "Webhook Secret", "Secret Key", or "Signature Key"
  5. Copy that value and paste it above
